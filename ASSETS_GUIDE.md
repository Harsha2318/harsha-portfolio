### 1. Profile Photo (`profile.jpg`)
**Location**: `assets/profile.jpg`
**Purpose**: Main hero section avatar
**Requirements**:
- Dimensions: 800x800px (square)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File size: < 500KB
- Professional headshot
- Clean background (solid color or minimal)
- Good lighting
- Facing camera
- Smile (professional)

**Tips**:
- Use a professional photographer if possible
- Ensure high resolution
- Neutral or branded background color
- Business casual or professional attire
- Center your face in the frame

---

### 2. About Image (`about-img.jpg`)
**Location**: `assets/about-img.jpg`
**Purpose**: About section illustration
**Requirements**:
- Dimensions: 1200x800px (3:2 ratio)
- Format: JPG
- File size: < 800KB
- Professional photo or workspace shot
- Can show you coding, in office, or professional setting

**Ideas**:
- You at your workspace with laptop
- Professional photo in different pose
- Speaking at an event
- Working on a project
- Team collaboration shot

---

### 3. Project Screenshots (`project1.jpg` to `project6.jpg`)
**Location**: `assets/project1.jpg`, `project2.jpg`, etc.
**Purpose**: Project portfolio cards
**Requirements**:
- Dimensions: 1200x800px (3:2 ratio) each
- Format: JPG
- File size: < 600KB each
- Clear, high-quality screenshots
- Show main interface or key features
- Good contrast and readability

**How to capture**:
1. Open your project/website
2. Use full-screen browser
3. Use tools like:
   - **Mac**: Cmd + Shift + 4
   - **Windows**: Snipping Tool or Win + Shift + S
   - **Chrome Extension**: Awesome Screenshot
4. Crop to highlight main content
5. Resize to 1200x800px
6. Compress with TinyPNG

**If you don't have 6 projects yet**:
- Use mockups or wireframes
- Include course projects
- Add personal experiments
- Feature open-source contributions
- Hide extra cards by removing them from HTML

---

### 4. Testimonial Photos (`testimonial1.jpg` to `testimonial3.jpg`)
**Location**: `assets/testimonial1.jpg`, `testimonial2.jpg`, `testimonial3.jpg`
**Purpose**: Testimonial avatars
**Requirements**:
- Dimensions: 200x200px (square)
- Format: JPG
- File size: < 100KB each
- Professional headshots of people who gave testimonials
- Get permission to use their photos

**Alternatives if you don't have testimonials**:
- Use avatar placeholders
- Create generic professional avatars
- Use illustration avatars
- Remove testimonials section temporarily
- Ask mentors, professors, or colleagues for testimonials

---

### 5. Favicon (`favicon.svg` or `favicon.png`)
**Location**: `assets/favicon.svg`
**Purpose**: Browser tab icon
**Requirements**:
- Format: SVG (preferred) or PNG
- Dimensions: 32x32px minimum (if PNG)
- Simple, recognizable design
- Works well at small size
- Your initials or logo

**Create using**:
- Canva (free)
- Figma (free)
- Favicon.io generator
- Adobe Illustrator
- Simple graphic editor

**Simple ideas**:
- Your initials (H, HP)
- Simple geometric shape
- Minimalist icon related to tech
- Your logo if you have one

### 6. Resume PDF (`Harsha_P_Resume.pdf`)

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is Key**: Use similar lighting and style for all photos
2. **Brand Colors**: Match image backgrounds to your color scheme
3. **Professional Quality**: Invest in good photos - they make a huge difference
4. **Compress Everything**: Large images slow down your site
5. **WebP Format**: Consider converting to WebP for better compression
6. **Retina Ready**: 2x images for high-DPI displays (optional)
7. **Alt Text**: Always add descriptive alt text in HTML
8. **Test Loading**: Check how images look while loading
9. **Mobile Optimization**: Ensure images look good on mobile
10. **Update Regularly**: Keep project screenshots current

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I use different file names?**
A: Yes, but you'll need to update the file names in `index.html`.

**Q: What if I don't have professional photos?**
A: Use your best available photos, or take new ones with good lighting and a clean background using your phone.

**Q: Can I use illustrations instead of photos?**
A: Absolutely! Illustrations can look very professional. Try Undraw.co or DrawKit.io.

**Q: How do I create a favicon from my initials?**
A: Use Favicon.io's text-to-icon generator: https://favicon.io/favicon-generator/

**Q: Should I use PNG or JPG?**
A: Use JPG for photos (smaller file size), PNG for graphics with transparency.

**Q: What about WebP format?**
A: WebP offers better compression. Convert your images but keep JPG fallbacks for older browsers.
